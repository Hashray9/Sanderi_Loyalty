============================================================
         SANDERI LOYALTY - TECHNICAL CONCEPTS USED
============================================================
Last Updated: 2026-02-08

=== BACKEND (packages/api) ===

--- Core Technologies ---
- Express.js: REST API framework
- TypeScript: Type-safe JavaScript
- Prisma ORM: Database abstraction and migrations
- PostgreSQL: Relational database

--- Authentication ---
- JWT (jsonwebtoken): Token-based authentication
- bcryptjs: Password hashing

--- Validation ---
- Zod: Schema validation for request bodies

--- Scheduled Jobs ---
- node-cron: Scheduled tasks (point expiry)

--- Database Seeding ---
- prisma/seed.ts: Initial database population (franchisee, stores, staff)
- prisma/seed-test-cards.ts: Test card seeding for development (3 active cards with points)

--- Key Concepts ---
- Idempotency keys: Prevent duplicate point entries
- FIFO point expiry: Points expire after 12 months
- Multi-franchisee support: Cards belong to specific franchisees
- Card lifecycle: UNASSIGNED → ACTIVE → BLOCKED

=== MOBILE APP (apps/mobile-new) ===

--- Core Technologies ---
- React Native: Cross-platform mobile framework
- Expo: Development and build tooling
- TypeScript: Type-safe JavaScript

--- State Management ---
- React Context: Auth and app state
- React Query: Server state management

--- Navigation ---
- React Navigation: Screen navigation

--- NFC ---
- react-native-nfc-manager: NFC card reading

--- Forms ---
- React Hook Form: Form handling
- Zod: Schema validation

=== DATABASE SCHEMA ===

--- Key Models ---
- Franchisee: Business entity owning stores
- Store: Physical location with conversion rates
- Staff: Employees with roles (ADMIN/EMPLOYEE)
- Card: NFC loyalty cards with point balances
- CardHolder: Customer linked to card
- PointEntry: Transaction ledger

--- Enums ---
- CardStatus: UNASSIGNED, ACTIVE, BLOCKED
- StaffRole: ADMIN, EMPLOYEE
- PointCategory: HARDWARE, PLYWOOD
- TransactionType: CREDIT, DEBIT, EXPIRY, VOID, TRANSFER
- BlockReason: LOST, STOLEN, FRAUD, OTHER
